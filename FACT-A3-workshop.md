## Welcome to Designing with AI, Not Around It

This hands-on workshop equips faculty with strategies to integrate generative AI into course design and assessment while preserving academic rigor and learner agency.

## Learning Outcomes

By the end of the workshop you should be able to
-  Explain why AI policies need pedagogical design
-  Align AI use with learning goals and learner needs
-  Distinguish helpful AI scaffolding from harmful shortcuts
-  Map course topics into Concept, Foundation, Thinking, and Application (FACT)
-  Design checkpoints that make student process, judgment, and accountability visible
